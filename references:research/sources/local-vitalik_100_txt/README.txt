Vitalik Buterin - 100 representative deep statements (summary pack)

What is inside
- 100 separate .txt files
- each file contains: title, channel, source, tags, and a detailed Chinese summary note
- channels covered in this pack:
  1) Vitalik personal blog (GitHub-backed public blog repository)
  2) Ethereum Foundation blog
  3) Ethereum Research

Method
- Priority was given to representative essays and research-style posts that are frequently cited in
  discussions around Ethereum scaling, decentralization, governance, legitimacy, cryptography,
  public goods, AI, privacy, and institutional design.
- Where a source was directly available in full, the summary was anchored to the source page.
- Where only stable title/snippet/source metadata was available during compilation, the summary was
  written as an editorial reconstruction based on the available context and Vitalik's broader corpus.

Important note
- These files are summaries and study notes, not verbatim reproductions.
- For precise wording, equations, edge cases, and original caveats, please open the source links.
